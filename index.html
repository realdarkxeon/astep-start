About me

I'm a software engineer from Kazakhstan. I work as one of the core team members of React, the popular JS frontend framework.

I'm also the creator of Eventy, a platform that connects the dots between user groups, speakers, conferences and attendees.

My passions are open-source, growing communities, and building products that people love.

Together with my buddies Maksat and Arman I organize events for Kolesa in the cities of Almaty and Shymkent. And we also organise Full Stack Central Asia, the conference for the whole team. This is where I share all the content I create. I mostly write articles and create courses, focusing on breaking concepts down to their fundamentals.

What do I do?

From school years, I have been interested in programming. I started with Python and then moved to C++. I have been working on various projects, from simple console applications to complex web applications.

Besides programming, I have a skill of Public Speaking. I have been giving speeches in various events and conferences. I have also been a part of the organizing team of the largest IT conference in Kazakhstan, KolesaConf.
